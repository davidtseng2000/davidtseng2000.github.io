About

Hello! I am 曾雋恩 (Tseng, Chun-En). I completed my Master’s degree in the Department of Computer Science at National Tsing Hua University, where I was part of the Algorithm and Bioinformatics Laboratory.

My research focused on the project titled “A Study of Multiple Reference Scaffolding Problem Based on Single-Cut-or-Join Distance,” which is a part of the field of Computational Biology. During my Master’s, I worked on the development of the EBD-scaffolder web server tool, which allows users to scaffold a target genome and visualize the results. This tool has contributed to research in biotechnology. Apart from my interest in Computational Biology, I have also explored topics such as Deep Learning, Data Mining, Graph Mining, Parallel Programming, Computer Graphics, and Fintech, through various courses.

This blog serves as a platform for me to share my learning experiences and daily life. Thank you for visiting, and I hope you have a wonderful day!
