About

Hello! 我是 David Tseng，畢業於國立清華大學資工所碩士班，Algorithm and Bioinformatics Laboratory。

我的研究論文為 ＜基於單切斷或重接距離的多參考支架問題研究＞，提出了兩種用來解決多參考基因體的 scaffolding （生物資訊）演算法。在學期間，我和學長合作做出了一套名為 EBD-scaffolder 的 web server，該工具可以幫助使用者解決單參考基因體的 scaffolding 問題，並且將結果以視覺化的方式呈現。

除了研究領域之外，我也曾修習深度學習、資料探勘、平行程式、計算機圖學、金融科技的相關課程。

這個部落格目前沒有固定的主題，想 po 啥就 po 啥。謝謝你 & 歡迎你來逛逛，祝你有美好的一天😺

Hello! I’m David Tseng, a graduate of the Master’s Program in Computer Science at National Tsing Hua University, Algorithm and Bioinformatics Laboratory.

My thesis, “A Study of Multiple Reference Scaffolding Problem Based on Single-Cut-or-Join Distance”, proposed two algorithms for solving multi-reference genome scaffolding problems in bioinformatics. During my studies, I also collaborated with my senior to develop EBD-scaffolder, a web server that helps users perform single-reference genome scaffolding and visualize the results.

Apart from my research, I have also taken courses related to deep learning, data mining, parallel programming, computer graphics, and financial technology.

This blog does not follow a specific theme—I simply post whatever I feel like sharing. Thank you for visiting, and I hope you have a wonderful day! 😺
